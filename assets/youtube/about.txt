RayMol is molecular visualization built for Apple Silicon — a Metal-based build of the open-source PyMOL engine, native on Mac, iPad, and iPhone. Real-time rendering, a modern multitouch interface, and Raymond, an AI copilot that helps you load, style, and analyze structures just by asking.

This channel covers feature demos, tutorials, and tips for visualizing proteins, ligands, and complexes — from publication figures to exploring a structure on your iPad.
