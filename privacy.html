Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Raymol does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Raymol has no user accounts, no sign-in, and includes no analytics, tracking, advertising, or crash-reporting that sends data to us or to third parties. We do not have servers that receive your data.

Your files stay on your device

Molecular structures, sessions, images, and any other files you open, create, or save are processed locally on your Mac, iPad, or iPhone. They are never transmitted to us.

Fetching structures from the Protein Data Bank

If you use Raymol's "Fetch from PDB" feature, the app sends only the structure identifier you request (for example, a 4-character PDB ID) directly to the public RCSB Protein Data Bank in order to download that publicly available structure. This request goes to the RCSB PDB, not to us, and is handled under the RCSB PDB's own policies. Raymol sends no personal information as part of this request.

Children's privacy

Because Raymol collects no personal data from anyone, it collects no personal data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@raymol.io.
